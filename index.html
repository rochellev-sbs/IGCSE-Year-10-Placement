Understanding Your IGCSE Mathematics Pathway
IGCSE Core Mathematics
Average below 70%

Core is a focused, well-structured curriculum that builds mathematical confidence from the ground up. It covers all the essential topics — number, algebra, geometry, statistics, and probability — with clear explanations and enough depth to develop genuine problem-solving skills.

Core is not a lesser path. It is the right environment for students who grow best with solid foundations before taking on added complexity. The pace is deliberate by design, allowing full understanding before moving forward. Many strong mathematicians and successful professionals started exactly here.

"Core Mathematics is for students who deserve the space to build real, lasting understanding — a thoughtful and structured path to mathematical competence."
IGCSE Extended Mathematics
Average 70% and above

Extended covers the same IGCSE Mathematics curriculum as Core, but with additional advanced topics layered on top — including more complex algebra, functions, vectors, and calculus-readiness content. It moves at approximately 50% faster pace, covering a wider range of material in the same timeframe.

Students placed here have shown strong, consistent performance across all four quarters. Extended is designed for learners who are ready to be challenged beyond the core content and who thrive when pushed to think more abstractly and independently.

"Extended Mathematics rewards students who have mastered the essentials and are ready to move faster, go deeper, and take on bigger mathematical ideas."
